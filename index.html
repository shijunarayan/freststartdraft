About us

Our Passion

At Frest Start, we are passionate about helping students around the world fulfill their dreams of studying abroad. Our goal is to provide you with all the resources and information you need to make an informed decision about where to study, what to study, and how to prepare for your adventure overseas.

Our Team

We are a team of passionate educators and travel enthusiasts dedicated to helping students experience the world through education. Our aim is to provide students with life-changing opportunities to learn, grow, and explore different cultures and perspectives through studying abroad.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with life-changing international experiences through cultural immersion, academic excellence, and personal growth. We strive to provide personalized and affordable study abroad programs that broaden perspectives, enhance intercultural communication skills, and foster global citizenship.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of transformative study abroad experiences that inspire individuals to become globally-minded leaders. We envision a world where every person has the opportunity to study abroad and gain a deep understanding and appreciation for diverse cultures and perspectives, creating a more interconnected and peaceful global community.

Services

We offer a comprehensive range of services to help students have a successful and enjoyable study abroad experience.

Program Advising

Study abroad companies often offer program advising services to help students choose the right program based on their interests, academic goals, and budget. This includes information on different study abroad options, program requirements, and eligibility criteria.

Application Assistance

Study abroad companies can assist students with the application process, including filling out forms, submitting required documents, and preparing for interviews.

Visa Support

Study abroad companies can provide guidance and assistance in obtaining the necessary visa to study abroad. This includes advice on the visa application process, requirements, and timelines.

Housing Assistance

Study abroad companies can help students find suitable accommodation, whether it is a dormitory, homestay, or apartment. They can also assist with lease agreements and arranging airport transfers.

Orientation Programs

Study abroad companies often organize pre-departure and on-site orientation programs to help students adjust to their new environment. This includes information on cultural norms, safety tips, and local resources.

Academic Support

Study abroad companies can provide academic support to students, such as arranging for language classes or tutoring services. They can also help students transfer credits and fulfill academic requirements.

Travel Assistance

Study abroad companies can help students plan their travel arrangements, including booking flights, arranging travel insurance, and providing information on local transportation.
